

## Recommended Features for Your Platform

Based on the current codebase analysis, here are high-impact features that would improve your platform:

### 1. **Search & Filter System (High Priority)**
- Homepage pe search bar with filters (category, language, year)
- SEO keywords jo abhi add kiye hain unko bhi search mein use karna
- Auto-complete suggestions while typing

### 2. **Movie Rating & Reviews**
- Users movie ko 1-5 star rating de sakein
- Short reviews/comments section on movie detail modal
- Average rating badge on movie cards

### 3. **Watchlist / Favorites**
- Users apni favorite movies save kar sakein
- Dashboard mein "My Watchlist" section
- Quick add button on movie cards

### 4. **Push Notifications System**
- New movie upload hone par notification
- Withdrawal status update notification
- Subscription expiry reminder (3 days before)
- Admin can send broadcast announcements

### 5. **Creator Analytics Dashboard Enhancement**
- Per-movie download graph (daily/weekly/monthly)
- Earnings breakdown chart by movie
- Top performing content highlights
- Comparison with previous period

### 6. **Social Sharing with Referral Tracking**
- WhatsApp/Telegram share buttons on movie cards
- Share link mein referral code embed karna
- Track which shared links brought downloads

### 7. **Admin: Bulk Actions & Reports**
- Bulk approve/reject withdrawals
- Export CSV reports (earnings, users, downloads)
- Weekly automated summary email to admin

### 8. **User Badges & Gamification**
- Badges for milestones (100 uploads, 1000 downloads, etc.)
- Leaderboard for top uploaders
- Profile badge display

### My Top 3 Recommendations (Priority Order):
1. **Search & Filter** — users ko content dhundhne mein sabse zyada madad milegi
2. **Watchlist/Favorites** — user engagement badhega
3. **Creator Analytics Enhancement** — creators ko retain karne mein help karega
